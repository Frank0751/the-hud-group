Our Mission

Transformed Lives.
Renewed Communities.

HuD Charity is the non-profit division of The HuD Group. We invest in people, developing leaders who in turn transform their communities, workplaces, families, and nations. Our work is rooted in the conviction that lasting social transformation begins with transformed individuals.
