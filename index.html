Who We Are

The Human
Development Group

The HuD Group (The Human Development Group) is a dynamic, international, Christian not-for-profit network and movement focused on the development of holistic Emerging and Established Leaders.

We are active in 30 countries across the globe, and are headquartered in Canada, with our Africa operations base in Accra, Ghana. Our work flows from the conviction that transformed leaders transform nations.

Our Vision
In every sphere, movements of inspired, informed and invested people of godly influence, accelerating God's purposes on earth.
Our Mission
To engage hearts, enlighten heads and empower hands of emerging and established leaders as we inspire, equip and connect them to discover, develop and deploy their God-given identity, purpose and resources to reach their full potential as holistic persons with maximum impact in every sphere as effectual missional communities.

Founded by
Visionary Leaders

The HuD Group was founded in 2003 by a remarkable team of co-founders who shared a conviction that transformed leaders transform nations. Over 23 years, that vision has grown into a movement spanning 30 countries across 6 continents.

From its roots in Ghana to a truly global network, The HuD Group continues to be led by the same spirit of excellence, faith, and holistic development that its founders instilled from day one.
